About Me


I am an assistant professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISysE) at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). Previously, I was a post-doc and a Young Scientist Fellow (YSF) in the Discrete Mathematics Group at the Institute for Basic Science (IBS) hosted by Sang-il Oum. I obtained my Ph.D. in the Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization (ACO) program at the Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Gerard Cornuejols. My thesis won the Gerald L. Thompson Doctoral Dissertation Award for the best thesis in Management Science, and I was supported by Henry J. Gailliot Presidential Fellowship.


Research Interests


Optimization: Integer programming and Combinatorial optimization.
