About Me


I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization (ACO) program at the Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University. 
I am very fortunate to be advised by Gerard Cornuejols. 
I am also lucky to work with Ahmad Abdi and 
Yanjun Li.
 

In the summuer of 2017, I worked at IBM Research as an intern under the guidance of Sanjeeb Dash and
